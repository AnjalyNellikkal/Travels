GOLDEN TRIANGLE WITH AMRITSAR

Detailed Itinerary


01: Arrive Delhi

Today arrive Delhi; the national capital of India. Upon arrival at airport, our company representative will meet & great you and transfer to the hotel for check-in. Overnight at Delhi hotel

Day 02: Delhi – Full day tour (Old & New Delhi tour)

Today morning have breakfast in the hotel. At 0930 hrs, the tour guide will meet you at your hotel and later proceed for full day guided tour in Delhi starting with old Delhi visiting Raj Gaht, Jama Maszid, drivepass through Red Fort. Later visit Humayun’s Tomb, India Gate, drive pass through Parliament Street & President house and visit Qutub Minar. Overnight at Delhi hotel

Day 03: Delhi – Agra (220 Kms. / 4.5 hours)

Today morning have breakfast in the hotel. Later drive to Agra, the city of “Taj Mahal”. En-route stop at Sikandra for a short photoshot. Later check-in to the hotel. Afternoon, proceed for half day guided tour in Agra visiting Taj Mahal & Agra Fort. Rest of the day free for shopping & own activities. Overnight at Agra hotel

Day 04: Agra – Jaipur via Fathehpur Sikri (240 Kms. /5 hours)

Today morning post breakfast, drive to Jaipur; the capital city of Rajsthan state. Jaipur is also known as “Pink City”. En-route visit Fathehpur Sikri, known as Ghost Capital. Later continue the drive to Jaipur. Upon arrival, check-in to the hotel. Evening free at leisure for own activities. Optional: Visit to Chokhi Dhani Village (US$ 25 per person). Overnight at Jaipur hotel

Day 05: Jaipur – Full day tour

Today morning after breakfast, we will proceed for full day guided tour in Jaipur starting with a short photoshoot at Hawa Mahal. Later enjoy the Royal Elephant Ride to ascend the Amber Fort. After visiting the Amber fort, return back to the city and visit the City Palace & Jantar Mantar observatory. Post tour; free for own activities and last minute shooing. Overnight at Jaipur hotel

Day 06: Jaipur – Delhi (256 kms. / 05 hours)

Today morning post breakfast, drive back to Delhi. Upon arrival, check-in to the hotel. Evening free at leisure. Overnight at Delhi hotel

Day 07: Delhi – Amritsar (By Shatabdi Train at 0740/1325 hours)

Today morning post breakfast, transfer to Delhi railway station to board the train to Amritsar. Upon arrival in Amritsar, transfer to the hotel for check-in. Afternoon proceed for half day guided tour in Amritsar visiting Golden Temple – built in the midst of the holy pool by Maharaja Ranjit Singh. We also visit Jaliawala Bagh. Evening we will take an excursion to Wagah border. Wagah border, also called the “Berlin wall” of Asia, is a ceremonial border on the India-Pakistan Border where each evening there is a retreat ceremony called 'lowering of the flags'. At that time there is an energetic parade by the Border Security Force (BSF) of India and the Pakistan Ranger soldiers. Overnight at Amritsar hotel

Day 08: Amritsar – Delhi (By Shatabdi Express at 1530/2145 hours)

Today morning post breakfast, day free at leisure for own activities and visit to Golden Temple. Afternoon, we will transfer you to Amritsar railway station to board the train to Delhi. Upon arrival in Delhi, transfer to the hotel for check-in. Overnight at Delhi hotel

Day 09: Delhi – Departure

Today on time transfer to Delhi International Airport (Indira Gandhi International Airport) to board the flight to onward journey.
