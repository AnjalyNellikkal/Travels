Punjab

The fertile land of farms and forts, Punjab has been aptly nicknamed the 'Smiling Soul of India' where you will always see smiles on the faces of the inhabitants who take pride in their religion and culture.There are many Forts in the state that tell the tale of the land's past. The Golden Temple, The Wagah border, The Sheesh Mahal are some of the major tourist attractions in Punjab.

Rajasthan

We have many happy memories of this trip as it was so varied but we especially loved being out in the countryside, camping in the desert and the cities of Udaipur and Jaisalmer. We also really enjoyed the wide range of accommodation: comfortable homestays in peaceful locations, beautifully painted havelis, grand palaces and of course glamping!
