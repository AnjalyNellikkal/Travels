GOLDEN TRIANGLE WITH KASHMIR

Detailed Itinerary


01: Arrive Delhi

Today arrive Delhi; the national capital of India. Upon arrival at airport, our company representative will meet & great you and transfer to the hotel for check-in. Overnight at Delhi hotel

Day 02: Delhi – Fly to Srinagar (By Morning Flight)

Today morning post breakfast, transfer to Delhi Domestic airport to board the flight to Srinagar. Upon arrival, transfer to the houseboat. Afternoon visit to Mughal Garden, Nishad Bagh & Shankracharya Temple. Evening free at leisure in houseboat. Overnight at Houseboat

Day 03: Srinagar – Excursion to Gulmarg (60 Kms – 01 way)

Today after early breakfast proceeds for full day excursion trip to Gulmarg 2730 Meters above sea level. Gulmarg A huge cup-shaped meadow, lush & green, with slopes where the silence is broken only by the tinkle of cow-bells, Gulmarg looks like a fantasy set in a film, and not surprisingly, has been the venue of several films. All around are snow-capped mountains, and on a clear day you can see all the way to Nanga Parbat in one direction & Srinagar in another. After lunch, have bridle walks and feed your cameras with the picture of magnificent view of mountains Enjoy horse ride (OPTIONAL) or gondola Cable Car ride (INCLUDED). Later drive back to Srinagar and spend evening time in houseboat bargaining with local sales man for handicrafts and Kashmir souvenirs. Overnight at Houseboat

Day 04: Srinagar – Delhi (By Afternoon Flight)Delhi – Agra (220 Kms. / 04 hours)

Today morning we will start our day with a Shikara ride; one of the most soothing & relaxing aspects of a holiday in Kashmir. Early morning before the sunrise visit the floating vegetable market in a Shikara. This unique kind of market can be only witnessed in Thailand and Kashmir. En-route to market, watch many species of aquatic birds on lakes, nestling on surrounding trees on floating marshes used for growing vegetables by locals. Return back to houseboats for breakfast. Later check-out and transfer to Srinagar airport to board the flight to Delhi. Upon arrival in Delhi, drive to Agra, the city of Taj Mahal. Overnight at Agra hotel

Day 05: Agra – Jaipur via Fethehpur Sikri (240 Kms. / 05 hours)

Today morning post breakfast, proceed for half day guided tour in Agra visiting Taj Mahal & Agra Fort. Afternoon check-out from the hotel and drive to Jaipur; the capital city of Rajasthan State. Jaipur is also known as "Pink City”. En-route stop at Fathehpur Sikri for a short photoshoot. Later continue to Jaipur. Upon arrival in Jaipur, check-in to the hotel. Optional: Visit to Chokhi Dhani Village (US$ 25 per person). Overnight at Jaipur hotel

Day 06: Jaipur – Full day tour

Today morning after breakfast, we will proceed for full day guided tour in Jaipur starting with a short photoshoot at Hawa Mahal. Later enjoy the Royal Elephant Ride to ascend the Amber Fort. After visiting the Amber fort, return back to the city and visit the City Palace & Jantar Mantar observatory. Post tour; free for own activities and last minute shooing. Overnight at Jaipur hotel

Day 07: Jaipur – Delhi (256 kms. / 05 hours)

Today morning post breakfast, drive back to Delhi. Upon arrival, check-in to the hotel. Evening free at leisure for own activities and shopping. Overnight at Delhi hotel

Day 08: Delhi – Full day tour (Old & New Delhi tour)

Today morning have breakfast in the hotel. At 0930 hrs, the tour guide will meet you at your hotel and later proceed for full day guided tour in Delhi starting with old Delhi visiting Raj Gaht, Jama Maszid, drivepass through Red Fort. Later visit Humayun’s Tomb, India Gate, drive pass through Parliament Street & President house and visit Qutub Minar. Overnight at Delhi hotel

Day 09: Delhi – Departure

Today on time transfer to Delhi International Airport (Indira Gandhi International Airport) to board the flight to onward journey.
