GOLDEN TRIANGLE WITH RANTHAMBORE

Detailed Itinerary


Day 01: Arrive Delhi

Today arrive Delhi; the national capital of India. Upon arrival at airport, our company representative will meet & great you and transfer to the hotel for check-in. Overnight at Delhi hotel

Day 02: Delhi – Full day tour (Old & New Delhi tour)

Today morning have breakfast in the hotel. At 0930 hrs, the tour guide will meet you at your hotel and later proceed for full day guided tour in Delhi starting with old Delhi visiting Raj Gaht, Jama Maszid, drivepass through Red Fort. Later visit Humayun’s Tomb, India Gate, drive pass through Parliament Street & President house and visit Qutub Minar. Overnight at Delhi hotel

Day 03: Delhi – Agra (220 Kms. / 4.5 hours)

Today morning have breakfast in the hotel. Later drive to Agra, the city of “Taj Mahal”. En-route stop at Sikandra for a short photoshot. Later check-in to the hotel. Afternoon, proceed for half day guided tour in Agra visiting Taj Mahal & Agra Fort. Rest of the day free for shopping & own activities. Overnight at Agra hotel

Day 04: Agra – Ranthambore via Fathehpur Sikri (240 Kms. /5 hours)

Today morning post breakfast, we will drive to Ranthambore National Park. En-route we will visit Fathehpur Sikri, Also know as Ghost Town. Later continue to Ranthambore National Park; one of the largest and most famous National Park in North India. Ranthambhore national park is famous for its tigers and is one of the best places in India to see these majestic predators in the Jungle. Tigers can be easily spotted even during the day time. Upon arrival, check-in to the hotel and rest of the day at leisure. Overnight at Ranthambore Resort

Day 05: Ranthambore – Jungle safari to the National Park

Today early morning before breakfast we leave for Jungle Safari. Later come back to the hotel for breakfast. Afternoon proceed for one more jungle safari to the national park. Safaris are carried out at two times: 0630 and 1430. Each ride lasts for about 3 hours.Evening you can visit Ranthambore Fort, built in 10th-century. Overnight at Ranthmabore Resort

Day 06: Ranthambore National Park – Jaipur (145 Kms. / 03 hours)

Today morning post breakfast, drive to Jaipur; the capital city of Rajasthan state. Jaipur is also known as “Pink City”. Upon arrival, check-in to the hotel. Evening free at leisure for own activities. Optional: Visit to Chokhi Dhani Village. Overnight at Jaipur hotel

Day 07: Jaipur – Full day tour

Today morning after breakfast, we will proceed for full day guided tour in Jaipur starting with a short photoshoot at Hawa Mahal. Later enjoy the Royal Elephant Ride to ascend the Amber Fort. After visiting the Amber fort, return back to the city and visit the City Palace & Jantar Mantar observatory. Post tour; free for own activities and last minute shooing. Overnight at Jaipur hotel

Day 08: Jaipur – Delhi (256 kms. / 05 hours)

Today morning post breakfast, drive back to Delhi airport. The total distance is 256 kms and you can cover it in 5 hours. Upon arrival Delhi, board flight to onward journey.
